❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How are the pet demand and value ratings calculated?

Our values are a mix of expert analysis and community input. We have a dedicated Value Team that constantly monitors active trading hubs, which is combined with live player votes to ensure the data reflects the actual state of the game.

What do the demand statuses (like "Hyped" or "Low") mean?

These statuses give you instant trading advice on a pet's current speed:

  • 🔥 Hyped: The pet is in massive demand and can easily fetch overpays right now.
  • 👍 High: The pet is in good demand and wanted by community. Not as good as hyped.
  • ⚖️ Medium: The pet has steady value and trades for fair, predictable offers.
  • 📉 Low: The pet is not mostly wanted by the community, often gets traded for underpays.
  • 🗑️ Terrible: The pet is not wanted by the community, always gets traded for underpays and rarely gets fair.

How often is the trading advice updated?

Market trends change fast in Adopt Me! Our Value Team updates the platform daily and sometimes hourly during major event updates so you never rely on outdated values.

Can I trust the community votes against market manipulation?

Yes! While anyone can vote, our system runs advanced filtering to block spam. Furthermore, our Value Team reviews all major trend spikes to ensure a small group of players can't artificially inflate a pet's value.
